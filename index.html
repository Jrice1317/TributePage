1. Robert F. Smith


  • Born: December 1, 1962 (59yo) ~ Denver, CO
  • Known For: Founded Vista Equity Partners
  • Net Worth: $6.7 billion

Robert F. Smith is the richest African American in the United States of America with a net worth of 6.7 billion dollars. He graduated from Cornell University in 1985 after becoming a proud member of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. He then went to work for Goldman Sachs in 1994 after earning his Masters from Columbia. After working six years in technology investing banking, Smith founded his own private equity firm Vista Equity Partners, which would go on to make over 50 billion dollars in assets and 22% in returns annually.




2. David Steward


  • Born: July 2, 1951 (70yo) ~ Chicago, IL
  • Known For: Founded World Wide Technology
  • Net Worth: $5.8 billion

Though born in Chicago, Illinois, David Steward was raised through segregation in Clinton, Missouri, where he graduated high school before he earned his BS in business at Central Missouri State University in 1973. Steward then worked in sales for several years at multiple companies before founding World Wide Technology in 1990. In 1999, its telecommunications division split to form Telcobuy.com. WWT, a systems integrator company, has gone on to generate over $11 billion in sales according to Forbes.




3. Oprah Winfrey


  • Born: January 29, 1954 (68yo) ~ Kosciusko, MS
  • Known For: The Oprah Winfrey Show, OWN Network, and The Color Purple
  • Net Worth: $2.6 billion

Oprah Gail Winfrey, born in Mississippi, was raised in Milwaukee before moving to Nashville, Tennessee, and becoming a co-anchor for the local news at the age of 19. Her delivery landed her her own daytime talk show, which broadcasted from Chicago, Illinois. In 2008, she formed her on Network, the Oprah Winfrey Network (OWN) and endorsed Barack Obama in his presidential race before receiving the Presidential Medal of Freedom in 2013. At one point, Winfrey was the ONLY black billionaire in the world.
